<p>I’ve always enjoyed traveling with my wife whenever we can. One thing I kept running into was how hard it is to find “hidden gems” or less obvious places, like small villages, lesser-known hiking spots, or vibrant new neighborhoods in big cities. Most platforms show the same popular places. That’s why I built Youtravel.</p>

<p>Youtravel is a responsive web app that lets you explore geolocated YouTube travel videos directly on a map. The idea is simple: instead of searching places, you explore real experiences. It’s useful both for planning trips and for those moments when you just wonder: “has anyone ever been here?”</p>

<ol>
    <li>Make travel discovery more exploratory and less list-based</li>
    <li>Use video content as a way to understand places</li>
    <li>Visualize content geographically through an interactive map</li>
    <li>Allow users to save and revisit interesting locations</li>
</ol>

<h2>Features</h2>

<h3>1. Map-based video exploration</h3>

<ul>
    <li>Browse videos from a list or directly on a map with pins</li>
    <li>Interactive map built with Leaflet</li>
    <li>Video previews inside map popups</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Smart video search</h3>

<ul>
    <li>Search geolocated videos using the YouTube API</li>
    <li>Filter by topics like food, hiking, budget travel or history</li>
    <li>Location suggestions powered by Geoapify</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Handling geolocation</h3>

<ul>
    <li>Use video metadata when available</li>
    <li>Fallback to tags and descriptions to infer locations</li>
    <li>Graceful handling of incomplete or missing data</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. User features</h3>

<ul>
    <li>Save favorite videos (when logged in)</li>
    <li>Access a personal favorites section</li>
    <li>Switch between English and Spanish</li>
    <li>Fully responsive for mobile use</li>
</ul>
